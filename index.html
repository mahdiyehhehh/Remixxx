The Room

Coffee. Music. Culture.

Everything on the counter and on the walls answers to one of three names. Nothing here is filler.

01 — Coffee

Coffee

Espresso pulled with intent, signature lattes built to be remembered, and enough matcha and frappes to make it through a Lahore afternoon.

02 — Music

Music

A wall of vinyl, a rotary phone that still works, and a booth that turns into a DJ set the moment the sun goes down.

03 — Culture

Culture

Comics on the counter, local art on the panelling, and a room designed to be sat in slowly — not rushed through.
